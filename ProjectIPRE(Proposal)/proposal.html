High Concept

Project IPRE follows the format of a classic shoot 'em up game with heavy influences from space and sci-fi genres. In this game the player must fight to outrun The Hunger before it consumes The Starblaster and ends thier research of the cosmos.

Genre

Project IPRE is an Sci-fi Action/Adventure 2D Shoot 'em up.

Platform

Web for desktop.

Story

After the IPRE crew successfully hid the Light of Creation for a decade, The Hunger supposedly lost its way in finding the precious artifact. The Hunger has now abandoned its original goal in favor of consuming everything. The IPRE team must find a way to flee The Hunger once again before its too late!

Aesthetics

Art

Vector shapes with clean lines - drawn to look sleek and fast. There will be art assets for The Starblaster, GUI, The Hunger, background, and The Light of Creation.

Audio

Epic syth cords with booming percussion to convey a fight for the future against an allconsuming enemy. There will be audio assets for The Starblaster, The Hunger, music, and UI.

Gameplay

Mechanics

The player must navigate around the screen using WASD controls and avoid the colorful circles while attempting to capture a bright white object called the Light of Creation. If the player retrieves the light, they recieve a temporary power up. If it falls past the bottom of the canvas, the Hunger consumes it and gains a permanent increase in both projectile speed and spawn rate. The objective of the game is to last as long as possible before succumbing to The Hunger.

Controls

Keyboard:

  • Enter: Start Game
  • WASD: Movement
  • Spacebar: Jump
  • P: Pause
  • Q: Game Guide

User Onboarding

This game is based off of a podcast called The Adventure Zone which is where a large majority of the userbase will come from. Therefor, it is not unreasonable to expect most users to understand the base story behind the game. However for those unfamiliar with the lore, there is a Game Guide which explains the premise of the game and how to play.

Player Learning

It is reasonable to expect the average user to be familiar with basic game mechanics such as WASD navigation and the concept of evading enemies. The basic controls are explained through the Game Guide screen. The game starts out very easy so that users can learn the controls and experiment a little bit before the difficulty picks up.
