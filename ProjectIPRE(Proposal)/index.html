Welcome to the Institute

Project IPRE is a shoot 'em up about a team of researchers that work for the Institute of Planar Research and Exploration. They are fleeing a cosmic entity know as The Hunger. They zoom away from aboard their trusty ship, The Starblaster.

About the developers

Declan Behan

My name is Declan Behan. I am a 2nd year Game Design & Development student attending Rochester Institute of Technology. I spend my days working towards my degree, designing games, writing Dungeons' & Dragons adventures, and producing music. I started my gaming journey in my small hometown of Jim Thorpe, Pennsylvania. I was launched into the virtual world of games with my trusty Nintendo DS and a twinkle in my eye. Now I am working towards my own D&D module, working on my second original soundtrack, and a secret 2D project.
